Got Questions?

Good cause we got answers!

> > Does reward have to be monetary? > 

While humans prefer money, you can offer anything (well, almost - our fun police are on patrol). You can even offer nothing if u want to test human kindness.

> > How do I post a referral request? > 

Click "Create Request" button and Presto, just fill in the fields! It's that simple! We even have step by step instructions if you need.

  Who can post a referral request?  

Anybody. Small business, medium business, large business, side business, project, or just a person who needs something. But all requests posted are subject to approval (within 24 hours) so please make it respectable. And Thank-you :).

  Who can answer & provide a referral?  

Anybody. We're all about being a helpful community. But please be respectful and provide relevant leads. And maybe you will make money or earn a reward along the way.

  Who sees my referral request?  

Right now everybody, but soon we’ll be introducing a feature that allows you the option to only post your referral request to only your Connections on this website. Conversely, today you can see either Everyone’s Request or Just Your Connections Request.

  How do I make money or reward?  

The idea is that when the referral you provided leads to a paying client, then u will be rewarded by the requestor. This will be at the discretion of the requestor and what was agreed upon between you two. Over time, as we introduce new features, you will be able to rate those who ask for referrals, how true they have stayed to their word, and we will track those who have provided rewards, which will encourage others to provide more referrals to them. Over time, an open and democratic system like this website result in a trustworthy referral based community.
