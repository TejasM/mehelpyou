PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction
This document (the "Privacy Policy") sets out MeHelpYou's privacy and personal information practices relating to your use of the MeHelpYou website (the "Site"). MeHelpYou may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and you are responsible for reviewing the most current version of this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy was last amended on the date shown at the top corner of this Privacy Policy page. If you do not agree with the Privacy Policy, you may not use the Site. Your continued use of the Site is taken to be your agreement in respect of the policies and procedures, and practices associated with this Privacy Policy and any updated that have been made. MeHelpYou considers personal information to be any information that can be used to identify an individual ("Personal Information").

Accessing to Personal Information
You can ask to view your Personal Information by contacting our privacy officer at privacy@MeHelpYou.com. Should you require that any corrections be made to your Personal Information you may request such corrections by sending a note to our privacy officer at privacy@MeHelpYou.com.

Registration
Once you have registered with the Site, you consent to the collection, use, disclosure and management of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Collection Practices
MeHelpYou collects personal information from you at the time of registration. The email address you provide will be used to send you updates and notification. Please note that any information that is contained in your user profile that you chose to make public through the use of the privacy settings found on the Site can be used and viewed by others, including through Internet search engines.

Third Party Services
Where you have chosen to link your account with other third party services such as Facebook, LinkedIn, and/or Twitter, you agree that your Personal Information may be shared with such third party services.

Use and Disclosure of Personal Information
Personal information collected by MeHelpYou will only be used or disclosed for purposes described in this Privacy Policy. We use and disclose your Personal Information for purposes of allowing you to engage with other users through submitting reviews and making connections on the Site. We may also use your Personal Information to customize the Site for you including through viewing of advertisements that are targeted to you. We do not disclose any of your Personal Information to third parties except as provided for in this Privacy Policy. We may disclose your Personal Information: (i) to third party service providers for purposes of our technical and hosting requirements; (ii) to MeHelpYou's affiliates that comply with the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy and (iii) to law enforcement agencies where such disclosure is required pursuant to law or for where we have a bona fide belief that we are aiding an investigation by law enforcement agencies.

Deleting your Account
Should you wish, you may request us to delete your account. Upon deletion of your account, your Personal Information will not be visible or viewable by anyone. However, records of your account, including your Personal Information may be stored by us pursuant to our record keeping and backup practices.
