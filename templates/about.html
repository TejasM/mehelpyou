MeHelpYou's Story

MeHelpYou aims to help professionals and businesses leverage their social networks and connections to the fullest. MeHelpYou believes that much of potential of obtaining information and leads of social networks is untapped. MeHelpYou was founded by Aazar Zafar, a professional investment manager and an entrepreneur, and Tejas Mehta, an upcoming entrepreneur and part of the Next36 2014 cohort.
